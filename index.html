Day-wise Itinerary (17–24 Aug, 2025)

Day 1 • Sun, 17 Aug — Chandigarh ➜ Shimla

~115 km • 4–5 hrs

Drive through the Shivalik Hills via Parwanoo & Solan. Ascend into cool pine forests and reach Shimla to soak in its colonial charm and ridge views.

Day 2 • Mon, 18 Aug — Shimla ➜ Sangla (Sightseeing)

~220 km • 7–8 hrs

Scenic drive via Rampur following the Baspa River. Visit Bering Nag Temple; stroll orchards and charming village lanes with Kinner Kailash views.

Day 3 • Tue, 19 Aug — Sangla ➜ Kalpa via Chitkul

Chitkul detour • Reckong Peo

Head to India’s last village, Chitkul. Continue through Reckong Peo to Kalpa. Visit Suicide Point, explore Roghi Village, and enjoy Kinnaur Kailash vistas.

Day 4 • Wed, 20 Aug — Kalpa ➜ Kaza (Sightseeing)

~200 km • High mountain roads

Traverse Nako Lake, Tabo & Dhankar Monasteries, and Pin Valley. Mountain drama all the way into Spiti’s stark landscapes.

Day 5 • Thu, 21 Aug — Kaza Local

Culture • Monasteries • Bridges

Explore Sakya Tangyud Monastery, then the 1,000-year-old Key Monastery. Drive to Chicham Bridge (among Asia’s highest). Shop at Kaza Market and unwind in cafés.

Day 6 • Fri, 22 Aug — Kaza ➜ Chandrataal (Sightseeing)

Via Losar • Kunzum Pass (4,551 m)

Ascend over Kunzum Pass with sweeping Himalayan views, then take the off-road track to the turquoise Chandrataal Lake. Camp beneath a starlit sky.

Day 7 • Sat, 23 Aug — Chandrataal ➜ Manali (Sightseeing)

Via Batal • Gramphu • Atal Tunnel

Descend the rugged Spiti roads via Batal and Gramphu, then glide through the Atal Tunnel to verdant valleys. Arrive in Manali for riverside cafés and lively markets.

Day 8 • Sun, 24 Aug — Manali ➜ Chandigarh (Airport Drop)

~310 km • 8–10 hrs

Drive along the Kullu Valley with scenic refreshment stops. Conclude with a comfortable airport drop in Chandigarh.
